A slower way
to begin the day

Tucked into a fold of the Ubud jungle, Villa Senja is a single hideaway for people who want less — fewer rooms, fewer sounds, more morning. No lobby, no welcome drink, no rush. Just mist on the valley and the smell of frangipani. The whole place is yours; it’s only ever let to one booking at a time.

We built one villa, not ten rooms — so the valley stays quiet enough to hear the gamelan drift up from the village at dusk.
